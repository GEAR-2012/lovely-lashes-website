Welcome to my page...

My story:

My Name is Elvira. I am from Hungary, but I stay in Scotland now for more than 10 years ago.

I had few different jobs before, but finally I found the best for me because I Love to do Lashes.

I received my first certificate in July 2015 and since then I welcome Ladies who want to be even more beautify. Initially I started to do eyelash extensions as hobby, then later it became my full-time job.

In March 2017 we opened our Salon in Aberdeen City Centre. Everyone can easily find us here, and also the parking is very simple as well.

In July 2017 I completed the advanced and master courses. Thereby I got the “Master Volume Certificate” and the “Lash Diploma” too in the Hungarian Lash Academy.

The thing that always drives me forward is my maximalism. I am working hard for years with the best supplies in order to provide the best quality to my Clients.

I Love my job, because the eyelash extensions emphasise the beauty of a glance. After the appointment I Love to see my Clients admiring themselves in the mirror with their sparkling eyes. Because they feel better in their skin.

I can promise you this: in the future with continuous progression, I will keep doing Lashes and customer service to my very best for my Lovely Loyal Clients.

Elvira x
